Faithful Merchant

Tutorial

Controls

It's really simple, just two buttons to play!
  • LMB (2x): Move ship to cursor
  • Spacebar (2x): Anchor ship to nearby settlement

Tutorial

You are the captain of a merchantman-class ship. Your task is to trade with various coastal settlements across the Indian Ocean. But your journey wouldn't be easy, a band of pirates are constantly chasing you all across the horizon.

Click around the map to set sail. If a settlement is open to trade, a coin would appear above them. You can press spacebar twice to anchor and trade with the nearest settlement.
